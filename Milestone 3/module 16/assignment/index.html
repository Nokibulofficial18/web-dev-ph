Our Packages

Know about Our Packages and Projects

Basic Package:

Target Audience: Homeowners looking for standard house designs or minor customization.

Features:

  • Pre-designed house plans
  • Basic customization options (e.g., room layout, facade color)
  • Simple 3D visualization of the design
  • Basic project consultation (1-2 hours)
  • Access to a gallery of existing designs
  • Downloadable PDF of the house plan

Price Range: $500- $2,000

Standard Package:

Target Audience: Clients looking for a more personalized design with moderate customization.

Features:

  • Semi-customizable house plans
  • Detailed 3D visualization and rendering
  • Initial site analysis (e.g., layout compatibility)
  • Consultation with an architect or designer (up to 5 hours)
  • Material selection guidance
  • Budget estimation for the design
  • Access to an expanded gallery of design ideas

Price Range: $2,000- $5,000

Premium Package:

Target Audience: Clients seeking a fully customized house design tailored to their specific needs.

Features:

  • Fully custom house design from scratch
  • In-depth consultation (up to 10 hours) with architects and designers
  • Comprehensive site analysis and compatibility report
  • High-definition 3D rendering and virtual walkthroughs
  • Unlimited revisions to the design
  • Interior design consultation and planning
  • Sustainable design options (e.g., eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient layouts)
  • Construction-ready blueprints and documents
  • Collaboration with contractors and engineers

Price Range: $5,000- $15,000+

Luxury Package:

Target Audience: High-end clients looking for exclusive, luxury home designs.

Features:

  • Bespoke architectural design services
  • Exclusive access to high-end materials and finishes
  • Full interior and exterior design with landscaping
  • Virtual reality walkthroughs of the design
  • Unlimited consultations and revisions
  • Coordination with top-tier contractors and suppliers
  • Project management support through the construction phase
  • Post-construction consultation (e.g., interior decorating, furnishing)
  • Smart home integration and technology consultation

Price Range: $15,000- $50,000+

Add-On Services:

  • Interior Design Package:
    Customized interior layouts, furniture selection, and decor.
  • Landscape Design Package:
    Custom landscaping and outdoor space design.
  • Sustainability Consultation:
    Advice on eco-friendly building practices and materials.
  • Home Renovation Design Package:
    Designs for home extensions, renovations, or remodeling.
  • Project Management:
    Ongoing management of the project through construction.
